Alumni/Alumnae Email List

The Department of Physics & Astronomy has created its own alumni email listserv for communicating with alums. Through this listserv, we will send occasional notes to you about what is happening in the Department. You will also be able to send out announcements to fellow alums. The listserv will be moderated to avoid spam messages.
